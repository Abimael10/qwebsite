First Resources Used

This was the first step I took to gain knowledge about this field. This is an amazing book written by J. J. Sakurai and Jim Napolitano which explains concepts that are going to make you feel more familiar with Quantum Mechanics concepts.

There are some math pre-requisites to be able to understand the author and the content written here, some of those would be:

  • Differential and Integral Calculus
  • Ordinary Differential Equations
  • Linear Algebra with Complex Numbers
